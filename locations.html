Villages

Hateno Village

Hateno village is the last populated village for hylians. This is also where link lives. Hateno village contains 1 of 2 Ancient Tech Labs accross hyrule. Hateno Village is one of the very few areas to not affected by the Great Calamity.

Kakariko Village

Kakariko Village is a village baced in the west necluda reigon, home to the remainder of the sheika tribe. The village specialises in Fortified pumpkins: A special type of pumpkin that grants low level defence when cooked.

Lurelin Village

Lurelin village is located in the outskirts of the faron reigon (south). The hylians who live here specialise in fishing and have caught many rare and highly nutrious food as they live only meters from the great necluda sea.

Civillisation

Tarrey Town

Tarrey town, being recently constructed in the akkala reigon by Hudson is a small town consisting of a mear 6 houses and a few over priced stalls. All types of races have made their home here.

Zora's Domain

Zora's domain is home to the race known as Zora. This half fish half human race have built their domain in a location surrounded by mountains made from luminous stone. Claimed by the devine beast Vah Rudania it is in constant rain.

Goron City

Goron city is home to the race of gorons. These strong rock like creatures live by a large volcanoe called Death Mountain. Due to the wrath of the devine beast Vah Rudania, gorons are having to constantly duck and cover as the beast triggers meteor showers.

Rito Village

Rito villiage, located near the hebra mountains, is the home of the bird species Rito. The Rito live in gazebo type houses perched around a large vertical rock. Claimed by deving beast Vah Medoh, the beast is constantly triggering large sawrms of wind.

Gerudo Town

Gerudo Town, located in the gerudo desert is home to the female only species: Gerudo. While only females are alowed in, Gerudo Town specialises in their market and the products to buy. Claimed by devine beast Vah Naboris, there are often large thunder storms.

Korok Forest

The korok forest, located in the woodland region is homw to all the korok children and The Great Deku Tree. With only 1 way of getting in, the korok forest is one of the most remote places on the map. You have to get through the lost woods without being consumed by the mist to only then be greeted by the games biggest secret. The last resting place of the legendary Master Sword

Devine Beasts

Vah Medoh

Devine beast Vah Medoh takes the form of a bird and is piloted by the Rito champion, Revali. Vah medoh is located above Rito village and in its un-controlled state generates massive gusts of wind upon Rito Village that makes living in the village an ordeal. Vah Medoh is home to the Wind Blight Ganon, the one who killed Revali

Vah Rudania

Devine beast Vah Rudania takes the form of a lizard and is piloted by the Goron champion, Daruk. This devine beast is located on death mountain and due to it being un-controlled, It sends large metiors raining down apon goron city and its residents. Vah Rudania is home to the Fire Blight Ganon, whom is responsible for Lord Daruk's death

Vah Ruta

Devine beast Vah Ruta takes the form of an elephant and is piloted by the Zora champion, Lady Mipha. Residing in Zoras Domain, The uncontrolled vah ruta has been consantly generating water and filling up the resivoir almost to the point of breaking and destroying Zoras Domain. Vah Ruta is home to the Water Blight Ganon, a creation made by the hands of the Great Calamity himself and the one responisible for Lady Mipha's perish.

Vah Naboris

Devine beast Vah Naboris takes the form of a cammel and is piloted by the Gerudo champion, Lady Urbosa. Vah Naboris is located in the Gerudo Desert near Gerudo town and the Kara Kara Baazar. Due to the beast being un-controlled, it creates large thunder storms posing a threat to those residing in Gerudo Town. Vah Naboris is home to the Thunder Blight Ganon, whom is responsible for Lady Urbosa's demise.
